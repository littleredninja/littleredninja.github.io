About Jennie

Jennie Chamberlin lives with her husband Joey and her precious cat, Bailey Boo. She hails from the lovely city of Virginia Beach but considers several places home, including Baltimore, Maryland; Kaneohe, Hawaii; Yokohama, Japan and most recently Comayagua, Honduras. She has loved photography all her life, works with film and digital platforms and collects cameras and camera-shaped things. She studied photography at the Maryland Institute College of Art in Baltimore and has worked as a professional photojournalist and writer since 2003.


Other loves include crime dramas, hiking, fiction books, contemporary art museums and conversations with friends and random strangers. She plays the piano horribly but likes to sing along anyway. She enjoys watching movies, making crafty things and eating big dinners. She adores traveling and loves to go places she's never been. She is always up for an adventure!
