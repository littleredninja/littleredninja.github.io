About Jennie

Hello! I'm Jennie and I'm an aspiring web developer and a student at Dev Bootcamp in Chicago, Illinois. I'm from the lovely city of Virginia Beach but consider several places home, including Baltimore, Maryland; Kaneohe, Hawaii; Yokohama, Japan and most recently Comayagua, Honduras.

I've loved photography all my life; I'm a user of both film and digital platforms and collect cameras and camera-shaped things. I studied photography at the Maryland Institute College of Art in Baltimore and have worked as a professional photojournalist and writer since 2003. I enjoy traveling and I love to go to places I've never been. I enjoy time spent with my husband Joey, my precious treasure cat Bailey and my family and friends. I'm always up for an adventure!
